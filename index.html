About Food Vision

Food Vision -has been a premier catering and full-service event planning company from 10 years, and is now one of the largest of its kind in Bangalore. Catering to affairs of all sizes, including corporate, social, gala and fund-raising events, institution and weddings, we provide professional service with a personal touch.

"Food Vision" offers Gourmet, Kosher, North - Indian, South Indian, Ethnic, Chinese, Punjabi, Continental & Italian cuisine, as well as full-service beverage, delivery, production, linen - decor services, and professional service.

Our Story

In 2004, Food Vision opened as a small set-up specializing to serve only institutions & minimal events. When the owners, Mrs. Shakun and Mr. Khalid started to grow the business, the little set-up grew quickly and soon began providing catering services for huge events.

In 2006, they had a vision for the company; they grew the business from catering operation into a full-service event company. And updated the brand, added “Catering, Banquet & Events”, to the company name, and continued to develop a classy reputation by providing restaurant-quality cuisine, impeccable service, and creating unique, custom events. Today, we have proudly served gatherings up to 4000 people at the same time and hope to serve many more.
